# Bookmark Store
A cross platform web application for storing bookmark.
Sometimes we save bookmarks in Laptop or PC and those bookmarks may be needed to access from Smartphone. It also happens that bookmarks that had been saved in Smartphone are needed to access from Laptop or PC. To do that we need to copy those link from Laptop or PC and send it to Smartphone and vice versa. But if you save bookmarks here you can access those bookmark from any device.
Just open this site and login to your account and then all your saved bookmarks will appear and you can browse those bookmarks.

Link: http://storebookmark.herokuapp.com/
